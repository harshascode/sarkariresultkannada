#### Box Office & Collection
War 2 started strong at the box office, collecting over ₹50 crore on its first day and seeing an increase on the second day. By the end of its first week, it had crossed ₹200 crore in India, a significant achievement. However, the film's collections dropped significantly in the second week, with the second Friday opening at around ₹4 crore and subsequent days showing minimal improvement. The total collection by the end of two weeks stood at approximately ₹225-226 crore net in India. Globally, the film is expected to finish with around ₹370 crore, a respectable figure but not enough to outdo other big releases like Rajinikanth's Coolie.

#### My Rating & Final Verdict
I would give War 2 a rating of 3/5. The film's inability to maintain its pace and engage audiences beyond its action sequences is a significant drawback. While it will be remembered for its star power and the historic coming together of Hrithik Roshan and Jr NTR, it falls short of being a truly memorable cinematic experience due to its narrative shortcomings. For fans of action films and those interested in seeing these stars share the screen, War 2 is worth a watch. However, viewers seeking a more balanced cinematic experience with compelling storytelling might find themselves slightly disappointed.
