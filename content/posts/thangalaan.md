## Thangalaan Review – A Historical Action Drama That Leaves a Lasting Impact

## Movie Summary & Story

Thangalaan, directed by the acclaimed Pa. Ranjith, is a historical action drama that has taken the Tamil film industry by storm. The film is based on the novel Ponniyin Selvan by Kalki Krishnamurthy and boasts a star-studded cast, including Vikram, Karthi, Jayam Ravi, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, and Trisha Krishnan. The movie is set in a bygone era and follows the story of Suryan, played by Vikram, a warrior with a complex past. The film's narrative is a perfect blend of historical fiction and commercial elements, making it a compelling watch.

The movie's setting is meticulously crafted, with grand scale, intricate set designs, and lavish visuals that transport the viewer to another age. The production design is flawless, and the cinematography by A. Kishor Kumar is stunning, capturing the grandeur of the period setting. The film's pacing is excellent, with a perfect balance of action, drama, and emotion, making it an engaging watch from start to finish.

## Technical Aspects

The technical aspects of the film are top-notch, with stunning cinematography that captures the grandeur of the period setting. The music, composed by G. V. Prakash Kumar, is catchy and complements the film's mood, while the background score is impressive, adding to the overall impact of the film. The editing, handled by Selva R. K., is seamless, and the pacing is excellent, making the film an engaging watch.

The film's soundtrack, which includes five songs, is another highlight. The songs are well-composed and add to the film's narrative, making them a memorable part of the viewing experience. The film's production design is also noteworthy, with grand sets and intricate details that bring the period setting to life.

## Performances & Characters

The performances in the film are outstanding, with Vikram delivering a remarkable portrayal of Suryan, a warrior with a complex past. His performance is both powerful and nuanced, bringing depth to the character. Malavika Mohanan shines as the strong-willed female lead, adding depth to the narrative. The supporting cast, including Daniel Caltagirone and Parvathy Thiruvothu, deliver commendable performances that add to the film's overall impact.

The characters in the film are well-crafted, with each one bringing their own unique perspective to the narrative. The film's cast delivers performances that are both convincing and engaging, making the characters relatable and memorable.

## Box Office & Collection

Thangalaan was released on August 15, 2024, and had a massive opening at the box office. The film's collections on the first day itself were unprecedented, setting a new benchmark for the Tamil film industry. Subsequent days also witnessed strong footfall, cementing Thangalaan's position as a commercial success. The film's budget, estimated to be between ₹100 to ₹150 crore, was recovered, making it a profitable venture for the producers.

## My Rating & Final Verdict

I would give Thangalaan a rating of 4.5/5. The film is a cinematic powerhouse that showcases the best of Tamil cinema. With its stunning cinematography, catchy music, and outstanding performances, Thangalaan is a must-watch for fans of historical action dramas. While the film's length might be a deterrent for some viewers, the engaging narrative and well-crafted characters make it a worthwhile watch. Overall, Thangalaan is a film that deserves to be watched on the big screen, and I highly recommend it to anyone looking for a compelling and engaging cinematic experience.
